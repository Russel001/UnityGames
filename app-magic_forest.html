1.0 What is Magic Forest Adventure?


1.1 It is a Match 3 game set in a medieval kingdom. You try to find and 'match 3' the different items needed by the kingdom namely:

1.1.1 Water- The 3rd most expensive item in the kingdom. It is drinking water.

1.1.2 Plant- The 6th most expensive item in the kingdom. It is plant cooking food and for other purposes.

1.1.3 Meat- The 4th most expensive item in the kingdom. It is cooking meat food.

1.1.4 Fruit- The 5th most expensive item in the kingdom. It is cooking fruit food.

1.1.5 Herb- The 2nd most expensive item in the kingdom. Fairies can give you free herbs if you are lucky.

1.1.6 Mineral- The most expensive item in the kingdom. Elves can give you free minerals if you are lucky.

1.1.7 Wood- The least expensive item in the kingdom. It is actually firewood.


1.2 As you try to find and 'match 3' the different items of the kingdom you will randomly be interrupted by:

1.2.1 Elves- Gives you minerals for free.

1.2.2 Fairies- Gives you herbs for free. More generous than elves.

1.2.3 Thieves- Will fight you if need be for your gold.

1.2.4 Orcs- Will fight you if need be for your gold. Tougher than thieves.

1.2.5 Crystal Ball Announcements- Announces items that are currently being priced higher and weapons/tools whose prices are being discounted.


1.3 You go to the kingdom to sell your items.


1.4 You go to the sorcery village to regain health.
