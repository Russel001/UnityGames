1.0 What is Pizza Delivery Girl?


It is an arcade style game set in a small road where the Player has to travel from one end of the road to the other while trying to avoid obstacles.

1.1 Introduction:
You are a Pizza Delivery Girl who has to deliver pizzas to the other side of the road. You would have to avoid bumping into obtacles such as people and cars so you don't spill your pizzas and lose points. If you spill/lose all your 10 pizzas then you lose the game. The more pizzas you deliver the higher is your score.

1.2 How the Player Moves Along the Game:
The Player moves along the game as if his/her finger is the feet of the Pizza Delivery Girl driving a skateboard.

1.3 How to Win:
You score a point/points when you deliver all or any remaining pizza you have on the building on the other side of the road which says 'Deliver Here'.

1.4 How the Game Ends:
The game ends when all your pizzas get spilled/lost as you bump into people and car obstacles.

1.5 High Scores:
The game automatically records the high scores for each level and displays them all in the level menu of the game.
